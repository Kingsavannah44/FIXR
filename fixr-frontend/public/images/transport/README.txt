FIXR TRANSPORT PAGE — IMAGE ASSETS
===================================

Drop your images in THIS folder with EXACTLY these filenames.
The Transport page (/transport) will load them automatically.

REQUIRED FILES:
---------------

HERO
  hero-bg.jpg            Wide road/highway shot, landscape orientation (1920x1080 recommended)

SERVICE CARDS (each 600x400 or 16:9 ratio)
  premium-ride.jpg       Clean saloon car, or smiling driver with passenger
  express-bolt.jpg       Car in motion / city speed blur
  local-matatu.jpg       Matatu / minibus on Nairobi street
  mpesa-pay.jpg          Person paying with phone / M-Pesa screen
  live-tracking.jpg      Phone showing map / GPS navigation app
  safety-first.jpg       Professional driver, seatbelt, safety uniform

HOW IT WORKS STEPS (each 800x500 or 16:9 ratio)
  step-open-app.jpg      Person opening FIXR app on smartphone
  step-choose-ride.jpg   Passenger selecting ride option on screen
  step-pay.jpg           M-Pesa payment confirmation / receipt on phone

WHY SECTION MOSAIC
  mosaic-driver.jpg      Driver behind the wheel, smiling, Kenya context
  mosaic-street.jpg      Nairobi street scene with vehicles / traffic
  mosaic-highway.jpg     Open highway, wide landscape (wide crop, ~1200x300)

TESTIMONIAL AVATARS (square, 80x80 minimum)
  avatar-1.jpg           Portrait of a man (Brian — gig worker)
  avatar-2.jpg           Portrait of a woman (Amina — SME owner)
  avatar-3.jpg           Portrait of a man (Kevin — engineer)

TIPS:
- JPG format preferred (smaller file size)
- Landscape photos work best for cards (wider than tall)
- Minimum 600px wide for card images, 1200px for hero
- The page gracefully shows a dark gradient if any image is missing
